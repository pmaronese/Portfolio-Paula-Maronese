Users

For all users who want to maintain a non-sedentary life; the age range that will use it the most will be 20 to 40 years.

Context

I am a student and a full time worker, both from home. That, although it has a few pros (especially in winter), it has a great disadvantage, which is being sitting all day long. Far from considering myself sendentary, I love sports and especially practicing new ones. I like to be on the go, but I also find motivation in knowing what I did, how long I did it, and how many calories I burned (even if I don't understand any calories, and eat at Mc').

For this reason, at this moment I have an app to do sports (and see the calories), a clock that reminds me that I have to get up and another app that reminds me to drink water every hour. Finally, I feel that the options to download are endless, but that none of them unites all these things that I consider essential for the proper functioning of our body, especially in times when most people are sitting in front of screens for many hours.
